# Salesforce Summer Program – Day 1

## 1. What is CRM?

**CRM** stands for **Customer Relationship Management**.  
It is a technology used to manage relationships with customers and prospects while tracking all interactions related to them.

CRM also helps organizations to:

- Collaborate internally and externally
- Gather insights from social media
- Track important business metrics
- Communicate through email, phone, social media, and other channels

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use **Salesforce** because it:

- Stores customer information centrally
- Improves communication with customers
- Helps track sales and opportunities
- Automates business processes
- Provides reports and analytics
- Supports customer service management

---

## 3. Accounts, Contacts, and Opportunities

### **Account**
An **Account** represents a company or organization you do business with.

### **Contact**
A **Contact** is a person who works at an account or company.

### **Opportunity**
An **Opportunity** represents a potential sales deal or business transaction.

**Example:**  
A company planning to purchase software services.
